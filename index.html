Claude Skill · Meetings & Icebreakers

Best Trivia Ever

Writes short, spoken trivia scripts for meeting icebreakers. Give it a question and answer — or ask it to help find a fact — tell it who's in the room, and it writes a tight, read-aloud script that builds curiosity and holds the answer until the reveal. It even fact-checks before handing it over.
